B.Voc Web Designing

B .Voc(Bachelor of Vocational Studies) is an emerging course among Delhi University.
It aims to provide adequate skills required for a trait to candidates,
The course is different from traditional academic programs as it is Industry based learning rather than focusing only on theoretical knowledge.

B. Voc is a 3 year duration Under Graduate course that can be pursued in wide range og fields but Kalindi offers it in Web Designing.
One of the biggest advantage of pursuing B.Voc against common degree course is that a candidate has multiple exit points during the program and continues Industry exposure.

This means that a candidate is unable to complete her B Voc course she is still awarded a diploma after completion of the first year and will get advance diploma after the completion of second year.
The students will also get certified from NASSCOM which has its own importance in the field of IT Industries which will also help to create opportunities for the students to get employed .

Course Curriculum

As stated above B.Voc is a three year duration course divided into six semester .The course curriculum of B.Voc (Web Designing) is Industry based which contains about 40% General Education(DU Subjects) & 60% Practical Components.
This means that the students will get appropriate opportunitiesto enhance their practical skills and make themself Industry ready.

Eligibility Criteria

A candidate must apply for the B.Voc along with DU Registration once they have successfully completed class 12'th from a recorgnized board along with Mathematics as main subject.
